Cambridge International Assessment Curriculum

Ditch the outdated classroom and join the future of learning! Earn a globally recognized Cambridge International qualification with a vibrant community of nearly a million learners across 160 countries. The Cambridge International curriculum isn't just another gold standard; it's an intellectual playground that stretches, challenges, and ignites your mind. Renowned for developing critical thinking skills, it's especially potent in math, science, and technology. Forget back-to-back lessons and wasted time. Here, learning is laser-focused and efficient. Start with IGCSE (starting January 2023) and graduate with expertise in three specialized subjects at 19 through International AS & A-Levels (starting January 2025).

CAPS

The South African Comprehensive Assessment Institute (SACAI) taps into the national curriculum and assesses students for the National Senior Certificate (NSC). This Matric qualification unlocks doors across South African universities and colleges. SACAI boasts accreditation from the Umalusi Council (number 22 AB04 00003) and offers learning in both English and Afrikaans

Free Curriculum

Forget the price tag, unleash your potential! Dive into a world-class education with our free curriculum, the gateway to endless possibilities. Join a vibrant community of learners across the globe, all fueled by the gold standard: the Cambridge International curriculum. No more back-to-back lectures, just focused exploration that ignites critical thinking, especially in math, science, and technology. Start your journey with IGCSE (beginning January 2023) and graduate with three specialized subjects at your fingertips by 19 through International AS & A-Levels (launching January 2025). It's your time to shine – and it won't cost you a dime.
