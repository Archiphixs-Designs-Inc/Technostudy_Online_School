Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Technostudy Online School?

A: Technostudy Online School is a virtual education institution that offers a wide range of educational programs, including the National Senior Certificate (NSC), Cambridge IGCSE, and Adult Matric Certificate (ASC). We provide high-quality online learning experiences for both primary and high school students.

Q: How can I enroll my child at Technostudy Online School?

A: Enrolling your child at Technostudy Online School is easy. Simply visit our website, create an account, choose the desired program, and follow the enrollment instructions. Our support team is also available to assist you throughout the process.

Q: Are there any discounts available for multiple siblings?

A: Yes, we offer a sibling discount. When you enroll more than one high school student, each additional sibling will receive a 5% discount on their fees.
